| p0-15 | Durak: original attacker never draws back up to 6 after a transfer | S | ✅ | **Shipped 2026-07-11, verified live** (`3ee87b4`). Fix: push instead of overwrite. *Done when:* a full transfer bout, once defended, redraws every seat that contributed a card (original attacker included) back to 6; regression test added; existing suite green. |
